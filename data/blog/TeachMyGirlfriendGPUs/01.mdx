Welcome to the first episode of the “Teach My Girlfriend GPU” series.
In this series, we’ll take a god’s-eye view of the GPU—not diving into code or specific APIs, but instead approaching it from the perspective of real-world needs, to understand how the GPU came to be, how it evolved, and why it works the way it does.

# The Origin of the GPU

Since the GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) truly emerged about 20 years ago, it has evolved from being a tool only used by professionals to a central topic of discussion in mainstream media. From gaming and film production to AI training and crypto mining, GPUs have deeply embedded themselves in every aspect of modern computing.
